ABOUT

I am a Research Associate at the University of Colorado, Boulder, United States. My research interest is galaxy formation and evolution. I'm working to explore how galaxies have formed and evolved with observational data obtained by state-of-art telescopes, for example, ALMA, Subaru, HST, and JWST.

I received a Ph.D. degree at the University of Tokyo under the supervision of Prof. Seiichi Sakamoto and the significant cooperation of the wonderful advisors, Dr. Yuichi Matsuda, Dr. Ken-ichi Tadaki, and Dr. Yuichi Harikane.

Currently I'm working with Dr. Wren Suess.

Sincerely grateful to everyone around me.

RESEARCH

My research interest is galaxy formation and evolution.

I'm investigating when and how galaxies form across the cosmic time with observational data obtained by large telescopes (e.g., ALMA, Subaru, HST, and JWST).
